What We Do

TempMail provides instant, disposable email addresses powered by the Guerrilla Mail free API. When you open our website, a unique email address is created for you automatically. You can use it immediately to receive confirmation emails, sign up for services, or test applications — without ever exposing your real email to potential spam or data harvesting.

Unlike many temporary email services, TempMail allows you to save multiple addresses directly in your browser. This means you can generate an address for a specific purpose, save it, and return days or weeks later to check for new messages. Your saved addresses are stored privately in your browser — they never leave your device.

Why Disposable Email Matters

Every time you sign up for a new website, subscribe to a newsletter, or register for a free trial, you typically need to provide an email address. This creates a paper trail that companies use to build marketing profiles, sell to data brokers, and flood your inbox with unwanted promotions.

According to Statista, spam emails account for over 45% of all email traffic globally. For many users, inbox management alone consumes hours of productive time each week. A disposable email solves this problem at the source — by keeping your real email address private.

Our Technology

TempMail is a completely static website with no backend server. Email delivery is powered by the free Guerrilla Mail API. All address storage happens in your own browser using the Web localStorage API — nothing is ever sent to our servers because we don't have any. This architecture means:

  • Zero data collection — we literally cannot see your emails
  • Maximum speed — no server round-trips for your data
  • Maximum privacy — your saved addresses never leave your device
  • 100% free — no infrastructure costs means no paid tiers

What TempMail Is Not

TempMail is a tool for privacy protection and convenience. It should not be used to deceive others, engage in fraudulent activities, or violate any platform's terms of service. We operate under a strict acceptable use policy and comply with all applicable laws. If you'd like to know more, please read our Terms of Service.

Our Commitment to You

We are committed to keeping TempMail free, fast, and private. We will never introduce account requirements, sell your data (since we don't have any), or put essential features behind a paywall. If we ever add optional premium features, they will be clearly labeled and the core service will remain free.
